- For informational purposes only
- Not a medical or healthcare service

### What We Are Not
- Medical professionals
- Licensed dietitians
- Healthcare providers

## Limitations

### Data Accuracy
- Nutrition data may have errors
- Menu items may vary by location
- Preparation methods may differ
- We cannot verify all information

### Individual Factors
- We don't know your full medical history
- We can't account for all conditions
- Medications may affect recommendations
- Genetic factors not considered

## User Responsibility

### Before Following Recommendations
- Consider your personal health conditions
- Consult healthcare provider if needed
- Use your best judgment
- Don't rely solely on our suggestions

### When to Seek Professional Help
- Existing medical conditions
- Taking medications
- Pregnant or breastfeeding
- History of eating disorders
- Under 18 years old

## No Guarantees

### We Cannot Guarantee
- Accuracy of all nutrition data
- Suitability for your specific needs
- Results from following recommendations
- Safety for all individuals

### Use at Your Own Risk
- Recommendations are suggestions only
- Final decisions are yours
- We are not liable for outcomes
- Always prioritize your health

## Emergency Situations

### If You Experience
- Allergic reaction
- Chest pain
- Difficulty breathing
- Severe symptoms

### Action
- Stop eating immediately
- Seek emergency medical help
- Contact emergency services
- Do not wait for our response

## Contact
For medical emergencies, contact your local emergency services immediately.
