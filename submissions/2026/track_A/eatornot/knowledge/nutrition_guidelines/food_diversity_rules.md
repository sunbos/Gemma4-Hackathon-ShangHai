# Food Diversity Rules

## Why Diversity Matters

### Nutrient Coverage
- No single food has all nutrients
- Different foods = different vitamins/minerals
- Variety prevents deficiencies

### Gut Health
- Diverse diet = diverse gut bacteria
- Better digestion and immunity
- Reduced inflammation

### Sustainability
- Prevents diet boredom
- More sustainable long-term
- Better relationship with food

## Food Groups

### Grains
- Rice, noodles, bread, oats
- Choose whole grains when possible
- 200-300g daily

### Protein
- Chicken, fish, pork, beef, eggs
- Tofu, beans, lentils
- 120-200g daily

### Vegetables
- Leafy greens, root vegetables
- Different colors = different nutrients
- 300-500g daily

### Fruits
- Fresh fruits preferred
- Limit juice (high sugar)
- 200-350g daily

### Dairy
- Milk, yogurt, cheese
- 300-500g daily

## Fast Food Diversity

### Problem
- Limited vegetable options
- High in processed ingredients
- Repetitive nutrition profile

### Solutions
- Rotate between different items
- Add available sides (corn, salad)
- Balance with diverse other meals

## Weekly Variety Target
- Aim for 12+ different food items per day
- 25+ different items per week
- Rotate protein sources
