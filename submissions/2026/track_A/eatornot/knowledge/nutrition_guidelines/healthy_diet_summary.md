# Healthy Diet Summary

## Core Principles

### Balance
- Eat a variety of foods from all food groups
- Balance calories in vs calories out
- Include protein, carbs, and healthy fats at each meal

### Moderation
- No single food is "bad" in moderation
- Portion control matters more than elimination
- Occasional treats are part of sustainable eating

### Variety
- Different foods provide different nutrients
- Rotate protein sources (chicken, fish, beans, tofu)
- Eat colorful vegetables and fruits

## Chinese Dietary Guidelines (2022)

### Daily Recommendations
- Grains: 200-300g (include whole grains)
- Vegetables: 300-500g
- Fruits: 200-350g
- Protein: 120-200g (fish, poultry, eggs, lean meat)
- Dairy: 300-500g
- Oil: 25-30g
- Salt: <5g

### Key Messages
- Eat breakfast daily
- Drink enough water (1500-1700ml)
- Limit sugary drinks
- Limit processed foods
- Cook at home when possible

## Fast Food in Context

### Acceptable
- Occasional fast food (1-2 times/week)
- As part of balanced weekly diet
- When time-constrained

### Strategies
- Choose grilled over fried
- Skip sugary drinks
- Add side salad if available
- Watch portion sizes
- Balance with lighter meals later
