# Nutrition Rules

## Daily Recommended Intakes (Adults)

### Calories
- Sedentary male: 2000 kcal
- Active male: 2500 kcal
- Sedentary female: 1600 kcal
- Active female: 2000 kcal

### Macronutrients
- Protein: 50-150g (10-30% of calories)
- Fat: 40-100g (20-35% of calories)
- Carbohydrates: 200-400g (45-65% of calories)

### Micronutrients
- Sodium: <2000mg recommended, >3000mg dangerous
- Sugar: <50g added sugar per day
- Fiber: 25-30g per day

## Food Quality Indicators

### Positive
- High protein (satiety, muscle maintenance)
- Low sodium (blood pressure)
- Low saturated fat (heart health)
- High fiber (digestion)
- Zero sugar drinks (vs sugary)

### Negative
- High sodium (>800mg per meal)
- High saturated fat (>10g per meal)
- Sugary drinks (>20g sugar)
- Deep fried items (trans fats)

## Fast Food Context
- Occasional fast food is acceptable
- Balance with lighter meals same day
- Choose grilled over fried
- Skip sugary drinks
- Watch portion sizes
