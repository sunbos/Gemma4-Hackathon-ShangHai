# Budget Rules

## Daily Budget Management

### Thresholds
- <50% of budget: comfortable
- 50-70% of budget: moderate
- 70-90% of budget: tight
- >90% of budget: warning

### Recommendations
- If <50%: any choice is fine
- If 50-70%: suggest value options
- If 70-90%: recommend lighter/cheaper
- If >90%: warn and suggest skipping or minimal

## Weekly Budget Management

### Tracking
- Sum all meals in rolling 7-day window
- Compare to weekly limit
- Factor in indulgence allowance

### Indulgence Cost
- Indulgence meals typically cost more
- Budget for 1-2 premium meals per week
- Balance with budget-friendly meals

## Value Metrics

### Price per Calorie
- Low cost, high calories = good value (filling)
- Low cost, low calories = budget friendly (light)
- High cost, high calories = indulgence
- High cost, low calories = poor value

### Price per Protein
- Higher protein per yuan = better value
- Important for weight loss (satiety)

## Fast Food Budget Tips
- Combo meals often better value than individual items
- Skip drinks to save ¥5-10
- Choose regular size over large
- Use coupons/promotions when available
